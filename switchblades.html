Switchblades

Thu 22 June 2023

By Ashley
Category: Homebrew Items

Description

A set of two that can be unfurled into a bladed whip-like arrangement. Allows the wield to swap between short ranged and long ranged fighting styles.

Stats

Furled: Deals 1d6 damage, Finesse, Light

Unfurled: Deals 1d4 damage, Range (10/20), Finesse, Light
